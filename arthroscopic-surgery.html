Precision Arthroscopic Surgery for Faster Recovery and Better Outcomes

Arthroscopic surgery is a minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose and treat joint problems through small incisions. It involves the use of a tiny camera (arthroscope) inserted into the joint to provide a clear, magnified view of the inside. Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S, a leading orthopedic surgeon in Bangalore, offers expert arthroscopic surgeries that result in less pain, reduced recovery time, and faster return to activity compared to traditional open surgery.

With state-of-the-art technology and specialized instruments, arthroscopy can treat a wide range of joint conditions in the knee, shoulder, hip, elbow, and ankle. Whether it's ligament reconstruction, cartilage repair, or joint debridement, our team provides precise, effective, and patient-focused care.

Arthroscopic Procedures We Perform

  • Knee Arthroscopy:

    • Meniscus repair/removal, ACL reconstruction, cartilage smoothing, and joint cleaning

  • Shoulder Arthroscopy:

    • Rotator cuff repair, labral tear repair, frozen shoulder release, impingement decompression

  • Hip Arthroscopy:

    • Treatment for femoroacetabular impingement (FAI), labral tears, and synovitis

  • Ankle Arthroscopy:

    • Debridement of scar tissue, removal of loose bodies, ligament repair

  • Elbow Arthroscopy:

    • Release of stiffness, removal of loose fragments, and treatment of tennis elbow

Advantages of Arthroscopic Surgery

  • Minimally invasive with smaller incisions and minimal tissue damage
  • Less post-operative pain and scarring
  • Faster healing and return to daily activities or sports
  • Precise diagnosis and treatment in a single procedure
  • Reduced risk of infection and shorter hospital stay

Some Common Questions

Have questions you want answers to?

A: Arthroscopic surgery uses a tiny camera (arthroscope) inserted through small incisions to visualize joint structures on a monitor. Miniature surgical instruments treat the identified pathology simultaneously. It is minimally invasive, offering less pain, smaller scars, and faster recovery than open surgery.

A: Knee arthroscopy treats meniscal tears, ACL and PCL ligament injuries, cartilage damage, synovitis, loose bodies, patellar instability, and early arthritis. Dr. Prasanna in Yeshwanthpur performs both diagnostic and therapeutic knee arthroscopy with excellent functional outcomes.

A: ACL reconstruction replaces the torn anterior cruciate ligament with a tendon graft (hamstring, patellar, or allograft) using arthroscopic technique. Tunnels are drilled in the femur and tibia to position the graft anatomically. Dr. Prasanna in Bangalore achieves near-normal knee stability post-reconstruction.

A: Shoulder arthroscopy treats rotator cuff tears, SLAP tears, shoulder instability (Bankart repair), AC joint problems, frozen shoulder, biceps tendon injuries, and impingement. Dr. Prasanna in Yeshwanthpur uses advanced arthroscopic anchors and suture techniques for precise, durable repairs.

A: Hip arthroscopy treats femoroacetabular impingement (FAI), labral tears, cartilage damage, loose bodies, and synovitis. It is a specialized procedure performed by Dr. Prasanna in Yeshwanthpur for patients with hip pain unresponsive to physiotherapy and injections, especially in young active patients.

A: Recovery depends on the procedure. Diagnostic arthroscopy and minor debridement allow return to activities in 1–2 weeks. ACL reconstruction requires 6–9 months for sports return. Meniscus repair takes 4–6 months. Dr. Prasanna provides structured rehabilitation protocols for all arthroscopic procedures.
