Do you experience persistent foot pain, difficulty walking, or foot deformities that affect your mobility and lifestyle? Your feet carry your entire body weight every day. Even minor issues can lead to discomfort, poor posture, and mobility challenges. At our orthopedic center, we combine precise diagnosis, evidence-based treatments, and state-of-the-art surgical techniques to ensure the best outcomes for patients of all ages.

Common Foot Surgeries & Treatments We Offer

  • Bunion Correction Surgery (Hallux Valgus):

    • Surgical realignment of the big toe joint to reduce pain and restore proper function.

  • Flat Foot Correction:

    • For patients with painful flat feet or collapsed arches; may involve tendon repair or joint realignment.

  • Plantar Fasciitis Treatment:

    • Advanced conservative therapies or minimally invasive procedures to relieve chronic heel pain.

  • Foot & Ankle Fracture Management:

    • Surgical and non-surgical treatment for traumatic injuries, ensuring proper alignment and healing.

  • Hammer Toe & Claw Toe Correction:

    • Surgical procedures to relieve deformities and prevent worsening.

  • Diabetic Foot Care & Ulcer Management:

    • Specialized care to prevent complications in diabetic patients with poor circulation or nerve damage.

When Should You See a Foot Specialist?

Consult a foot surgeon in Bangalore if you have:

  • Chronic foot or heel pain that doesn't improve with rest or medication
  • Visible deformities like bunions, flat feet, or toe abnormalities
  • Difficulty walking, standing, or wearing shoes
  • Numbness, swelling, or tingling in the foot
  • Foot injuries or fractures not healing properly

Some Common Questions

Have questions you want answers to?

Surgical foot conditions include bunions (hallux valgus), hammertoes, flatfoot deformity (pes planus), Charcot foot, midfoot arthritis, Morton's neuroma, metatarsalgia, and toe deformities. Dr. Prasanna in Yeshwanthpur evaluates each condition and recommends the most appropriate surgical approach.

Modern foot surgery uses advanced anesthesia and pain management protocols for a comfortable experience. Recovery depends on the procedure — minor corrections take 4–6 weeks, while complex reconstructions may take 3–6 months. Dr. Prasanna's team provides detailed post-operative care instructions and physiotherapy support.

Bunion surgery (bunionectomy) corrects the deviation of the big toe by realigning the metatarsal bone and removing the bony prominence. Minimally invasive techniques allow smaller incisions, less pain, and faster return to footwear. Dr. Prasanna performs this procedure routinely in Yeshwanthpur, Bangalore.

Flatfoot reconstruction involves a combination of tendon repair, bone cuts (osteotomies), and sometimes joint fusion to restore the foot's natural arch. It is recommended for severe flatfoot causing pain, instability, or deformity. Dr. Prasanna in Bangalore tailors the procedure to the patient's specific anatomy.

Yes, many foot procedures including bunion correction, hammertoe repair, and neuroma excision can be performed using minimally invasive or percutaneous techniques. These approaches reduce incision size, post-operative pain, and recovery time. Dr. Prasanna employs these methods at his Yeshwanthpur clinic.

Weight-bearing time depends on the procedure. Minor surgeries allow protected weight-bearing immediately, while complex reconstructions require 4–8 weeks non-weight-bearing. Dr. Prasanna provides a customized recovery plan and mobility aids to ensure safe, progressive ambulation after foot surgery.

Orthotics are the first-line treatment for many foot conditions. Surgery is recommended when deformity causes significant pain, functional limitation, or is structurally progressive. Dr. Prasanna conducts a thorough evaluation in Yeshwanthpur to determine whether conservative management or surgery best addresses your condition.

Many foot surgeries are performed as day-care (outpatient) procedures, allowing patients to return home the same day. More complex reconstructions may require a short hospital stay. Dr. Prasanna's clinic in Yeshwanthpur offers both outpatient and inpatient foot surgery options.
