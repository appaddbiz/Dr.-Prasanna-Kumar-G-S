Patient Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about robotic knee replacement, robotic hip replacement, arthroscopy surgery, recovery, and total knee replacement with Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S.

Robotic knee replacement surgery uses a robotic arm to assist the surgeon in placing the knee implant with greater precision, resulting in faster recovery, less pain, and longer implant life compared to traditional surgery.

Most patients start walking within 24 hours after robotic knee replacement. Full recovery typically takes 6–8 weeks, significantly faster than conventional knee replacement.

Yes, Dr. Prasanna Kumar offers robotic hip replacement surgery in Yeshwanthpur, Bengaluru, making it accessible for patients from Peenya, Jalahalli, and surrounding areas.

Arthroscopy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure where a small camera, called an arthroscope, is inserted into the joint to diagnose and treat conditions like ACL tears, meniscal tears, and cartilage damage.

Total knee replacement is recommended when knee arthritis causes severe pain, stiffness, and difficulty walking that does not improve with medications, physiotherapy, or injections.
