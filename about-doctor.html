Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S

Orthopedic and Joint Replacement Surgeon

Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S

Orthopedic Surgeon in Yeshwanthpur, Bengaluru

Dr. Prasanna Kumar GS is a highly skilled orthopedic surgeon with around 13 years of experience in the field. He specializes in treating a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, including dislocated joints, tendon injuries, arthritis, cerebral palsy, growth abnormalities in children, scoliosis, and post-surgical rehabilitation through physiotherapy. Dr. Prasanna is particularly proficient in joint replacement surgeries involving the knee, shoulder, hip, and elbow, and is adept at performing advanced robotic knee replacement procedures. His expertise also includes arthroscopic or keyhole surgeries such as ACL and PCL reconstruction, meniscus repair, loose body removal, shoulder arthroscopy, knee diagnostic arthroscopy, and ligament repair.

13 Years of Experiences

Dr. Prasanna began his medical education with an MBBS from Sri Siddhartha Medical College and Research Institute, Tumkur, in 2012. He pursued his MS in Orthopedics from Grant Government Medical College and Sir JJ Group of Hospitals, Mumbai, completing it in 2019. He further qualified with a DNB in Orthopedics from the National Board of Examination, New Delhi. To deepen his clinical expertise, Dr. Kumar underwent specialized training through fellowships in joint replacement and arthroscopy sports medicine. His dedication to excellence was recognized when he received the prestigious IOA AO Trauma Fellowship in 2022. In addition to his clinical practice, he has contributed to several publications in the field and is an active member of the Indian Orthopedic Association.
