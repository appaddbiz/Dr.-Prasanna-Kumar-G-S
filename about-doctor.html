Orthopedic and Joint Replacement Surgeon

Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S

Orthopedic Surgeon in Yeshwanthpur, Bengaluru

Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S is a highly accomplished and trusted Senior Orthopaedic, Joint Replacement, and Arthroscopy Surgeon known for his expertise, precision, and patient-centered care with more than 12 years of experience. With an outstanding academic and professional background, he has dedicated his career to helping patients regain mobility, live pain-free, and return to an active lifestyle with confidence.

12+ Years of Experience

He completed his MBBS from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore and pursued his MS in Orthopaedics from the prestigious Grant Medical College and Sir J. J. Hospital, Mumbai, one of India's most renowned high-volume orthopaedic centers. He further earned his DNB in Orthopaedics from the National Board of Examinations, Delhi, strengthening his academic excellence and specialized expertise in orthopaedic surgery. Recognized for his surgical skills and commitment, he continued to serve at the same institution for two additional years as an Orthopaedic Surgeon, gaining extensive hands-on experience in complex trauma and joint replacement and sports medicine surgeries.

To further refine his expertise, Dr. Prasanna completed a Fellowship in Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy and Sports Medicine from the esteemed Asian Joint Reconstruction Institute (AJRI), Chennai. He also underwent specialized AO Trauma Fellowship training and has been trained in advanced robotic joint replacement surgeries, bringing cutting-edge technology and modern surgical techniques to his patients.

With over 50 national and international publications in Orthopaedics, Dr. Prasanna is actively involved in academic research and evidence-based medical practice. His contributions to the field reflect his dedication to innovation and excellence in orthopaedic care.

Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S previously served for four years as a Joint Replacement and Arthroscopy Surgeon at Manipal Hospital Yeshwanthpur and is currently associated with Fortis Hospital Yeshwanthpur, where he continues to provide advanced treatment for knee, hip, shoulder, sports injuries, arthritis, ligament injuries, and complex orthopaedic conditions.

Patients trust Dr. Prasanna not only for his surgical excellence but also for his compassionate approach, accurate diagnosis, and commitment to delivering the best possible outcomes. Whether it is joint pain, sports injuries, arthritis, or the need for advanced joint replacement surgery, Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S offers world-class orthopaedic care tailored to every patient's needs. Book an appointment with Dr. Prasanna Kumar G S and take the first step towards a pain-free and active life.
